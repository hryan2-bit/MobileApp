    About

    Why would you use this app?

    If you ever feel like you or a your group cannot decide on something, and cannot decide what you want to eat or do not want to make someone have all of the pressure to decide the place to eat at for the group or yourself.


    How does it work?

    This app works by using all of the selections you have chosen. Adding them to a temporary list that is only available while you are currently using the app. Then, randomizing through the list in a near true randomization with no biases.
